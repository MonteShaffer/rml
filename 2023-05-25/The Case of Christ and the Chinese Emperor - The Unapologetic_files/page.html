Prophetic Word: Easy Will Lead to Death

"Micah, why do you look for other things to fill your heart?"

Jesus' direct question startled me as we stood on the top ...of large golden steps that led inside to Father's heart. I didn't know how to answer.

Jesus held my hand and looked at me kindly, "I will ask you again. Why do you look for other things to fill your heart?"

I had to say, "Jesus, sometimes loving you is hard. It is easier to find something that will satisfy you now. Why do you always make it hard to love you, Jesus?"

He pulled me close so our heads touched and sighed with his eyes closed, "I know it is not easy, Micah. I don't want easy; I want what is real. I ask that you give up everything for me and walk the way of fire because I am a consuming fire. I ask that you walk the path that few choose because easy is what will lead to death. Micah, easy, will be what people will leave me for. Are you going to leave me?"

Jesus touched my face tenderly, "I was left for a politician because he was easier. They left me for a movement because it gave quick answers. People leave me, Micah and many more will leave because they want it easy. I am good, Son. But narrow is the way; hidden is the path to find me."

Will you follow Jesus or leave him because you want it easy?
